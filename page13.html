Japanese Invasion

The Japanese invasion of the Philippines started on the 8th of December 1941, approximately 17 hours after the attack on Pearl Harbor (Pacific Atrocities Education, n.d.).

The main motivation behind the colonization was due to the Philippines' strategic location for oil transport.
