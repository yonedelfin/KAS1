Challenges in the 17th century

The Spaniards also faced numerous challenges in the 17th century which were mainly caused by resistance from the Filipinos. In 1621, there was a religious uprising against the Spanish led by Bankaw. Other local uprisings were a result of the unjust Bandala system, the establishment of pueblos, and the abuse of polo y servicio.
