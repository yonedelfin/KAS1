Precolonial Culture

Although the precolonial Philippines did not have an organized society and a united language, they had similar aspects of culture.

Early communities were described to have strong kinship and familial bonds as well as strong ties to the land and sea. Body adornment, vices, and feasting were also common practices in pre-colonial societies (Abinales & Amoroso, 2017).

Abinales, P. N., & Amoroso, D. J. (2017). State and Society in the Philippines. Rowman & Littlefield.
