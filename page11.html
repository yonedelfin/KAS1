The Benevolent Assimilation Proclamation

The United States government outlined its motivations and policies for occupying the Philippines in the Benevolent Assimilation Proclamation. In this document, President William McKinley proclaimed that the state and military government in Manila is to be extended to the Philippines and that the US government succeeds in the sovereignty of the Spanish government over the Philippines (Sumangil, 1976).

Sumangil, T. (1976, August). How the Philippines Became the First Colony of the United States. Fil-Minnesotan.
