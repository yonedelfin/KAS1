Precolonial Class Divisions

The Maginoo class was considered the elite class during the pre-colonial period. Their power was inherited and datuship also entitled military, judicial, religious, and entrepreneurial roles. They were supported and served by the Timawa or warrior class. The masses spent most of their lives in servitude with some being Aliping namamahay or Alipin sa gigilid.

Abinales, P. N., & Amoroso, D. J. (2017). State and Society in the Philippines. Rowman & Littlefield.
