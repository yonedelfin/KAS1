Life During the Japanese Occupation

Life in the Philippines was difficult during the Japanese occupation. Only one political party was allowed during the occupation which was the Japanese-organized KALIBAPI (Rottman, 2001).

Numerous war crimes against Filipino and American civilians were also documented during this time.
